Akan Name Generator

The application will take user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.

Akan menu

Tip:If you are a female and you were born in September 13th 1997 your Akan Nickname will be Ama. Incase of a male with the same details, your Akan nickname will be Kwame.
